Book Reviews

Top 10 Book Reviews, besides Jonas Čeika's, are one of my favorite ways of finding new concepts I would have normally never come across, even though they are technically still videos.

Of these Top 10 Books, I think Angela Davis' Are Prisons Oboslete? has to be my favorite. It was such a radically different perspective on something I had never even thought twice about, kind of like the critique of K-pop. There are a lot of books covered in this playlist, but if there were any I would reccomend to anyone reading this, it is definitely Are Prisons Obsolete?
